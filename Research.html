Runyi​ Yao


My DPhil project is investigating the causal inference and expectations in discourse processing. For example, I explore sentences like 'The injured man was hit by a car', and ask questions Why (or Why not, in some situations) and How people obtain an inference that the man was injured because he was hit by the car.    

Here are some findings of this project:
